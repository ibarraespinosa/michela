# Research

My research focuses on **bioinspired advanced manufacturing** — creating micro- and nano-scale devices that interact safely and effectively with the human body.

### Current Projects

- **Hollow Microneedle Arrays** — prototyping minimally invasive brain microinjection devices at the BAM Lab (UMD)
- **Soft Robotic Catheters** — hybrid 3D printing approaches for cardiac tissue repair applications
- **Polymeric Scaffolds** — biohybrid systems for tissue engineering and regenerative medicine

### Core Expertise

- Micro/nano-scale additive manufacturing (3D printing at the micron level)
- Surface modification of titanium alloys for biomedical implants
- Drug delivery coatings and bioactive glass composites
- Finite element modeling of manufacturing processes
- Laser texturing and ultra-short laser surface engineering
