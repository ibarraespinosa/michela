# Portfolio

A selection of my work at the intersection of advanced manufacturing and biomedical engineering.

*Portfolio images coming soon — placeholder gallery below.*

### Microneedle Arrays
Micro-scale hollow microneedle arrays for minimally invasive brain microinjections. Designed and prototyped using high-resolution additive manufacturing at the BAM Lab, UMD.

### Soft Robotic Catheters
Hybrid 3D-printed soft robotic catheter prototypes for *in situ* bioprinting applications in cardiac tissue repair.

### Polymeric Scaffolds
Biohybrid scaffold systems for tissue engineering — designed for cell culture integration and controlled degradation.

### Surface Modification of Titanium Implants
Laser-textured and mechanically modified Ti6Al4V surfaces engineered to modulate bone cell response and improve coating adhesion.
